Bear Ridge Loop

Payette National Forest, Idaho
Distance 6.2 mi
Difficulty Moderate
Image

Description

Bear Ridge Loop climbs through open ponderosa stands before breaking onto an exposed ridgeline with sweeping views of the Payette River valley. Expect steady switchbacks on the way up and a gentler, shaded descent through the eastern draw. Best hiked in the early morning during summer months — the ridge holds little shade after 10am.
